Biography

I am a graduating master’s student at UCL Computer Science. I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jun Wang, and have closely worked with Dr. Tommaso Pasini on my master’s research thesis. Before coming to UCL, I completed my B.S. in Computer Science and B.S. in Statistics with double minors in Mathematics and Information Systems and Statistical Analysis (now renames to Information Sciences and Technology in Communication Arts and Sciences and Labor and Employment Relations) at Penn State University.

My research interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain Economics, and Algorithmic Game Theory. My motivation is to create intelligent agents that can reason and cooperate like humans. I used to work on multimodal machine learning for drug discovery and generative arts, as well as applied deep reinforcement learning in energy management and autonomous driving. Now I am also interested in Web3 and its investment chances.
